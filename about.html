ABOUT

I have lived through many layers of life— theatre, cities, work, illness, and silence. There was a time I wanted to become someone else. In theatre, I could step into another life, and not have to be myself. But something in me knew. There were times I moved quickly, holding things together, trying to do everything well. And there were times I had to stop, and learn how to simply stay. Now, I draw, I write, and slowly build a space that feels like my own. This is not a place of answers, but a place to breathe, to see, and to be. I am no longer trying to become someone else. I am learning to be here.
我走過生命的許多層次—— 劇場、城市、工作、疾病，還有沉默。 曾經有一段時間，我想成為別人。 在劇場裡，我可以進入另一個角色， 暫時不用當自己。 但心裡有一個地方，一直知道。 曾經有一段時間，我走得很快， 努力撐住一切，把每件事做到最好。 也有一段時間，我不得不停下來， 學習只是待著，好好存在。 現在，我畫畫、寫字， 慢慢地，建構一個屬於自己的空間。 這裡不是為了給答案， 而是一個可以呼吸、觀看、存在的地方。 我不再試著成為別人， 我正在學習，好好地在這裡。
Sora Kai
